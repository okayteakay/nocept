## Phase 2 — Exception Detection and Classification Engine

### Step 5 — Three-way matching logic ✅
- ✅ `_compute_line_variances()` — per-line SKU/qty/price delta for all SKUs across invoice and PO
  - ✅ `is_new_sku=True` for invoice-only SKUs
  - ✅ `is_expedited_shipping=True` for SHIP-EXP or "expedited" description
  - ✅ `price_delta_pct` computed as fraction of PO unit price
  - ✅ `quantity_delta` as signed integer (invoice - PO)
- ✅ Configurable tolerance thresholds via `AppConfig` (`price_tolerance_pct`, `qty_tolerance_pct`)

### Step 6 — Exception classifier ✅
- ✅ `classify_exception()` in `agent/classifier.py` — full implementation:
  - ✅ Missing GRN detection → `MISSING_GOODS_RECEIPT`
  - ✅ Duplicate detection via supplier index scan → `DUPLICATE_INVOICE`
  - ✅ Price variance (per-line, configurable tolerance) → `PRICE_VARIANCE`
  - ✅ Quantity variance (per-line % of PO qty, configurable) → `QUANTITY_VARIANCE`
  - ✅ Informal modification signals → `INFORMAL_MODIFICATION`
- ✅ `_detect_informal_modification_signals()` — 5 heuristics:
  - ✅ New non-shipping SKU on invoice
  - ✅ Expedited shipping surcharge
  - ✅ Substitution swap pattern (shortfall + new SKU)
  - ✅ Product grade change on shared SKU
  - ✅ Pure price uplift ≥ 1% with no new SKUs
